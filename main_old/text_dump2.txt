We decomposed the massive problem into three distinct pillars, perfectly matching your 3-person team structure:

Pillar I (Vision & Healing): Fusing 5.8m LISS-IV Optical data with 10m Sentinel-1 SAR data using a dual-encoder SegFormer. This physically penetrates cloud cover ("spectral blindness") and produces a continuous probability mask using a topology-aware clDice loss function.
Pillar II (Skeletonization): Bridging computer vision and math. We use morphological thinning (Zhang-Suen) and the sknw algorithm to compress the road pixels into 1-pixel centerlines, automatically extracting a traversable NetworkX graph (nodes and edges).
